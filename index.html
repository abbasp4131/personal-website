The story

How I got here.

In 2023 I started a marketing agency with one plan: call gym owners until someone said yes. I cold-called 1000+ of them. Most hung up. A few shouted. One said yes, then ghosted. Another finally signed.

Somewhere around dial eight hundred, I realized gym owners weren't my people. Their problem was fuzzy and the ones who had it couldn't afford me. So I pivoted into real estate, where the math was cleaner and the offer landed harder.

That became Agent Accelerator, a performance-based agency booking qualified listing appointments for real estate teams and brokerages. Pay-per-appointment, tied to outcomes. We've partnered with 35+ agents at Compass, RE/MAX, Keller Williams, Sotheby's, eXp, Century 21, and Coldwell Banker. Managed $130K+ in YouTube and Meta ad spend. Held an 89% show-up rate on booked calls.

I still cold-call. Not because I have to. Because nothing keeps a founder honest like picking up the phone and hearing "not interested."

Now I'm looking for B2B sales or growth roles where the outcome matters more than the org chart. Startups preferred, but good people trump good logos.
